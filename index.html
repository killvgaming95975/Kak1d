🧼 Namaz Ki Taiyaari

💧 Wuzu Ke Steps (Paaki)

1 Bismillah padhein aur haath dhoye (kalahi tak) - 3 martaba
2 Kulli karein (munh mein paani daal kar ghumaayein) - 3 martaba
3 Naak mein paani daalein aur saaf karein - 3 martaba
4 Chehra dhoye (mathe se thodi tak, kaan se kaan tak) - 3 martaba
5 Daahina haath dhoye (kohni tak) - 3 martaba, phir baayaan haath
6 Sar ka Masah karein (geele haathon se sar par phero) - 1 martaba
7 Kaano ka Masah karein (ungli se andar bahar saaf karo) - 1 martaba
8 Daahina pair dhoye (takhno tak) - 3 martaba, phir baayaan pair

🤲 Niyat (Dil Ka Irada)

Dil mein irada karein (zuban se bolna zaroori nahi):

"Main niyat karta/karti hoon [2/3/4] rakat [Farz/Sunnat/Nafl] namaz ki, Allah ke liye, muh mera Kaaba shareef ki taraf."

🕋 Qibla Ki Taraf

Kaaba Shareef (Makkah) ki taraf muh karke khade ho jayein. India mein Qibla Paschim (West) ki taraf hota hai.

💡 Tip: Mobile mein "Qibla Finder" app use kar sakte hain.

🕌 2 Rakat Namaz

(Fajr ki Sunnat/Farz, Aur Koi Bhi 2 Rakat Namaz)

📿 Pehli Rakat

1️⃣ Takbeer-e-Tahrima (Namaz Shuru)

Dono haath kaano tak uthayein aur bolein:

"Allahu Akbar"

Phir haath baandh lein (naaf ke neeche ya seene par).

2️⃣ Sana Padhein

Subhanaka Allahumma wa bihamdika, wa tabarakasmuka, wa ta'ala jadduka, wa la ilaha ghairuk.

3️⃣ Taawwuz aur Tasmiyah

A'udhu Billahi Minash Shaitanir Rajeem

Bismillahir Rahmanir Raheem

4️⃣ Surah Fatiha Padhein (Poori)

Alhamdulillahi Rabbil 'Alameen. Ar-Rahmanir-Raheem. Maliki Yawmid-Deen. Iyyaka Na'budu wa Iyyaka Nasta'een. Ihdinas-Siraatal Mustaqeem. Siraatal Lazeena An'amta 'Alaihim, Ghairil Maghdooobi 'Alaihim Walad-Daalleen. (Ameen)

5️⃣ Surah Ikhlas Padhein (Poori)

Qul Huwallahu Ahad. Allahus-Samad. Lam Yalid Wa Lam Yoolad. Wa Lam Yakullahu Kufuwan Ahad.

6️⃣ Ruku Mein Jayein

"Allahu Akbar" bolte hue jhukein. Haath ghutno par rakhein, kamar seedhi.

Ruku mein 3 martaba padhein:

"Subhana Rabbiyal Azeem"

7️⃣ Qauma (Ruku Se Khade Hon)

Bolte hue khade hon:

"Sami Allahu Liman Hamidah"

"Rabbana Lakal Hamd"

8️⃣ Pehla Sajda

"Allahu Akbar" bolte hue sajde mein jayein. Pehle ghutne, phir haath, phir naak, phir maatha zameen par.

Sajde mein 3 martaba padhein:

"Subhana Rabbiyal A'la"

9️⃣ Jalsa (Dono Sajdon Ke Beech Baithna)

"Allahu Akbar" bolte hue baith jayein. Thoda rukein.

🔟 Doosra Sajda

"Allahu Akbar" bolte hue phir sajde mein jayein.

Sajde mein 3 martaba padhein:

"Subhana Rabbiyal A'la"

📿 Doosri Rakat

1️⃣ Khade Ho Jayein

"Allahu Akbar" bolte hue doosri rakat ke liye khade ho jayein.

2️⃣ Bismillah aur Surah Fatiha Padhein (Poori)

Bismillahir Rahmanir Raheem

Alhamdulillahi Rabbil 'Alameen. Ar-Rahmanir-Raheem. Maliki Yawmid-Deen. Iyyaka Na'budu wa Iyyaka Nasta'een. Ihdinas-Siraatal Mustaqeem. Siraatal Lazeena An'amta 'Alaihim, Ghairil Maghdooobi 'Alaihim Walad-Daalleen. (Ameen)

3️⃣ Surah Kawthar Padhein (Poori)

Inna A'taina kal Kawthar. Fa Salli Li Rabbika Wanhar. Inna Shani-aka Huwal Abtar.

4️⃣ Ruku Mein Jayein

"Allahu Akbar" bolte hue jhukein.

3 martaba padhein:

"Subhana Rabbiyal Azeem"

5️⃣ Qauma (Khade Hon)

"Sami Allahu Liman Hamidah"

"Rabbana Lakal Hamd"

6️⃣ Dono Sajde Karein

Pehle sajde ki tarah - har sajde mein 3 martaba:

"Subhana Rabbiyal A'la"

🪑 Aakhri Baithak (Qa'dah Akhira)

1️⃣ Attahiyat Padhein (Poori)

Attahiyatu Lillahi Wassalawatu Wattayyibatu. Assalamu 'Alaika Ayyuhan-Nabiyyu Wa Rahmatullahi Wa Barakatuhu. Assalamu 'Alaina Wa 'Ala 'Ibadillahis-Saliheen. Ash-hadu Alla Ilaha Illallahu Wa Ash-hadu Anna Muhammadan 'Abduhu Wa Rasuluh.

2️⃣ Durood Shareef Padhein (Poora)

Allahumma Salli 'Ala Muhammadin Wa 'Ala Aali Muhammadin Kama Sallaita 'Ala Ibraheema Wa 'Ala Aali Ibraheema Innaka Hameedum Majeed. Allahumma Barik 'Ala Muhammadin Wa 'Ala Aali Muhammadin Kama Barakta 'Ala Ibraheema Wa 'Ala Aali Ibraheema Innaka Hameedum Majeed.

3️⃣ Dua Padhein (Poori)

Allahumma Inni Zalamtu Nafsi Zulman Katheeran, Wa La Yaghfiruz-Zunooba Illa Anta, Faghfirlee Maghfiratan Min 'Indika Warhamnee Innaka Antal Ghafoorur Raheem.

4️⃣ Salam Pherein

Pehle daayein taraf muh kar ke bolein, phir baayein taraf:

"Assalamu Alaikum Wa Rahmatullah"

✅ Alhamdulillah! Aapki 2 Rakat Namaz Mukammal Hui!

🕋 Jumah Ki Namaz

(Jumu'ah - Har Jumme Zuhr Ki Jagah)

⚠️ ZAROORI BAAT:

Jumah ki namaz SIRF Masjid mein Imam ke peeche Jamat ke saath hoti hai.

Akele ghar mein Jumah ki namaz NAHI ho sakti. Agar masjid nahi ja sakte, toh 4 rakat Zuhr padhein.

📋 Jumah Ka Poora Sequence — Ek Nazar Mein

1

4 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah

Masjid pahunchte hi — Khutba se PEHLE padho

2

📢 Khutba — Chup Reh Kar Suno

Imam ka bayan — bilkul khamosh rehna zaroori hai

3

2 Rakat FARZ — Imam Ke Peeche ✅

Yeh Jumah ki farz namaz hai — zaroori

4

4 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah

Farz ke foran baad — bahut zaroori hai

5

2 Rakat Sunnat — Optional ✨

Padh sakte ho extra sawab ke liye

✅ Jumah Total: 4 + 2 + 4 + 2 = 10-12 Rakat

🔵 STEP 1 — 4 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah (Khutba Se PEHLE)

⏰ Kab padhe?

🔵 2. SUNNAT NAMAZ — Nabi ﷺ Ka Tareeqa

Sunnat padhne se bahut sawab milta hai. Chhorna gunah nahi (Muakkadah chhoda toh makrooh hai)

🤔 "Muakkadah" Kya Hota Hai? — Simple Explanation

📱 Ek Simple Example Se Samjho:

Socho tere boss ne kaha:
"Kaam pe aana zaroori hai" — yeh FARZ hai
"Thoda jaldi aao toh achha hai" — yeh MUAKKADAH hai
"Aur jaldi aao toh bhi theek hai" — yeh GHAIR MUAKKADAH hai
"Extra kaam karo toh bonus milega" — yeh NAFL hai

⭐ MUAKKADAH

"Mu-ak-ka-dah" = Pakka / Confirmed

✅ Matlab: Nabi ﷺ ne yeh sunnat HAMESHA padhi — kabhi nahi chhoodi

⚠️ Chhoda toh: Makrooh (bura) — gunah hoga

🎁 Padha toh: Bahut bada sawab milega

📌 Simple rule: Isko chhodo mat — Farz ke saath saath yeh bhi zaroori hai

🔹 GHAIR MUAKKADAH

"Ghair" = Nahi / "Pakka" = Confirmed

✅ Matlab: Nabi ﷺ ne kabhi kabhi padhi, kabhi nahi padhi

😌 Chhoda toh: Koi gunah nahi bilkul

🎁 Padha toh: Sawab milega

📌 Simple rule: Padh sako toh padho — extra sawab milega

💡 Beginner Ke Liye Simple Rule:

🔴 Farz = Bilkul zaroori — kabhi mat chhoodo
🟠 Sunnat Muakkadah = Almost zaroori — koshish karo hamesha padho
🔵 Sunnat Ghair Muakkadah = Optional — waqt ho toh padho
⚪ Nafl = Extra — jab chaaho padho

⭐ Sunnat Muakkadah — Kab Kitni?

Nabi ﷺ ne in sunnaton ko hamesha padha aur kabhi nahi chhooda. Inhe chhorna makrooh (bura) hai.

🌅 Fajr se pehle: 2 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah
☀️ Zuhr se pehle: 4 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah
☀️ Zuhr ke baad: 2 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah
🌆 Maghrib ke baad: 2 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah
🌙 Isha ke baad: 2 Rakat Sunnat Muakkadah

🔹 Sunnat Ghair Muakkadah — Optional

Nabi ﷺ ne kabhi kabhi padhi, kabhi kabhi nahi. Padho toh sawab, na padho toh koi gunah nahi.

🌇 Asr se pehle: 4 Rakat Sunnat Ghair Muakkadah
🌙 Isha se pehle: 4 Rakat Sunnat Ghair Muakkadah

⚪ 3. NAFL NAMAZ — Extra Ibadat

Nafl = Bilkul Optional. Padho toh bahut sawab, na padho toh koi baat nahi.

Yeh Extra ibadaat hai jo Allah ke qurb ke liye padhi jaati hai.

📌 Mashhoor Nafl Namazein:

🌙 Tahajjud

Raat ke aakhri hisse mein — 2 se 12 rakat tak. Bahut afzal namaz!

🌅 Ishraaq

Suraj nikalne ke 15-20 minute baad — 2 rakat. Hajj ke sawab ke barabar!

☀️ Chasht (Duha)

Dupahar se pehle — 2 se 8 rakat. Roz ke sadaqe ke barabar sawab.

🕌 Tahiyyatul Masjid

Masjid mein daakhil hone par — 2 rakat. Baithne se pehle padho.

💧 Tahiyyatul Wuzu

Wuzu karne ke baad — 2 rakat. Sawab bahut zyada hai.

💡 Nafl Kaise Padhe?

Same method — 2 ya 4 rakat. Niyat mein "Nafl" bolo. Niyat karte waqt koi specific Nafl ka naam bhi le sakte hain jaise "Tahajjud" ya "Ishraaq".

🤔 Sajda-e-Sahw Kya Hota Hai?

📖 "Sahw" ka matlab hai — bhool jaana

Namaz mein agar koi wajib cheez bhool jaao ya galti ho jaaye — toh Allah ne ek aasaan solution diya hai: 2 extra sajde — yeh hain "Sajda-e-Sahw" ✅

💡 Simple Samjho:

Jaise exam mein ek sawaal choot gaya toh bonus marks milte hain — waise namaz mein bhool ho toh Sajda-e-Sahw se woh "gap" fill ho jaata hai ✅

📋 Sajda-e-Sahw Kab Karna Hai?

⚠️ Yaad Rakho:

Sirf Wajib cheez bhoolne par Sajda-e-Sahw karna hota hai. Sunnat bhool gayi? Koi baat nahi — Sajda-e-Sahw nahi karna.

❶ Qa'dah Oola (Pehli Baithak) Bhool Gaye

📌 Kya hua?

3 ya 4 rakat namaz mein — doosri rakat ke baad baithna (pehla qa'dah) bhool gaye aur seedha 3rd rakat ke liye uth gaye

✅ Kya karo?

Namaz poori karo phir aakhir mein Sajda-e-Sahw karo

📱 Real Example:

Zuhr ki 4 rakat padh raha hoon. 2nd rakat ke 2 sajde kiye aur sidha 3rd rakat ke liye khada ho gaya — baitha hi nahi. Yeh bhool hai → namaz poori karo → end mein Sajda-e-Sahw karo ✅

❷ Attahiyat Mein Tashahud (Kalima) Bhool Gaye

📌 Kya hua?

Baithne ke waqt Attahiyat padhna bhool gaye ya adhuri padhi

✅ Kya karo?

Namaz mein jo reh gayi woh complete karo, aakhir mein Sajda-e-Sahw karo

📱 Real Example:

Aakhri baithak mein Attahiyat ke beech mein hi Durood padh liya — Attahiyat poori nahi thi. Yeh bhool hai → Sajda-e-Sahw karo ✅

❸ Witr Mein Dua Qunoot Bhool Gayi

📌 Kya hua?

Witr ki 3rd rakat mein Dua Qunoot padhna bhool gaye aur ruku mein chale gaye

✅ Kya karo?

Ruku se uthne ke baad Dua Qunoot wapis nahi padh sakte — sirf aakhir mein Sajda-e-Sahw karo ✅

❹ Rakat Kam Ya Zyada Ho Gayi (Shak)

📌 Kya hua?

Yaad nahi raha ki 3 rakat padhi ya 4 — shak ho gaya

✅ Kya karo?

Jo zyada mumkin lage woh rakaat maan lo aur namaz poori karo. Aakhir mein Sajda-e-Sahw karo.

Agar bilkul bhi andaza nahi toh kam rakaat maan lo (zyada safe hai) aur baaqi padh ke Sajda-e-Sahw karo.

📱 Real Example:

Zuhr ki 4 rakat padh raha hoon. 4th rakat mein baitha toh socha — "yeh 3rd hai ya 4th?" Zyada lagta hai 4th — toh 4th maan ke salam phero aur Sajda-e-Sahw karo ✅

🤔 Masbuq Kaun Hota Hai?

📖 "Masbuq" = Joh Late Aaya

Wo shakhs jo jamat shuru hone ke baad masjid pahuncha — aur kuch rakaat Imam ke saath nahi padh saka. Aise shakhs ko "Masbuq" kehte hain.

💡 Simple Rule Yaad Rakho:

Jo rakaat Imam ke saath padhi = woh POORI MILI ✅
Jo rakaat choot gayi = woh Imam ke salam ke baad KHUD POORI KARNI HAI ✅

📋 3 Common Situations — Examples Ke Saath

🔵 Situation 1: 1 Rakat Choot Gayi (3 Rakat Jamat Mein Se)

Example: Maghrib (3 Rakat) mein 1st Rakat choot gayi

📖 Kya hua?

Masjid pohunche tab Imam 2nd rakat mein tha. Toh 2nd aur 3rd rakat Imam ke saath padhi. 1st choot gayi.

✅ Kya Karo Step by Step:

1️⃣ Niyat karo Imam ke saath aur Takbeer kaho — namaz mein shamil ho jao

2️⃣ Imam ke saath 2nd aur 3rd rakat poori padho — Imam ke peeche khamosh raho

3️⃣ Imam salam pherte hai — tum salam mat phero! Uthte raho!

4️⃣ Khade ho jao aur 1 rakat khud padho (Fatiha + Surah + Ruku + 2 Sajde)

5️⃣ Baithok — Attahiyat + Durood + Dua + Salam ✅

⚡ Important:

Jab Imam salam pherta hai tab tum baithe rehte ho aur uthke apni chhooti rakat poori karte ho — jaise normally karte.

🟠 Situation 2: 2 Rakaat Choot Gayi (4 Rakat Jamat Mein Se)

Example: Zuhr (4 Rakat) mein 2 Rakaat choot gayi

📖 Kya hua?

Masjid pohunche tab Imam 3rd rakat mein tha. Sirf 3rd aur 4th rakat Imam ke saath padhi. 1st aur 2nd choot gayi.

✅ Kya Karo Step by Step:

1️⃣ Imam ke saath 3rd aur 4th rakat padho (Imam ke peeche khamosh)

2️⃣ Imam salam pherta hai — tum mat phero!

3️⃣ Khade ho jao — 2 rakat khud padho

→ 1st khud wali: Fatiha + Surah + Ruku + 2 Sajde

→ Baith jao — Attahiyat padho (sirf) — uthna hai abhi

→ 2nd khud wali: Fatiha + Surah + Ruku + 2 Sajde

4️⃣ Aakhri baithak — Attahiyat + Durood + Dua + Salam ✅

💡 Rule:

Khud padhi jane wali rakaat mein Surah Fatiha + koi Surah dono milao — jaisa pehli 2 rakat mein hota hai.

🔴 Situation 3: Sirf Ruku Ya Sajda Mila (Last Moment Aaye)

Example: Aaye tab Imam ruku mein tha

📖 Kya hua?

Masjid pohunche tab Imam ruku mein tha — Fatiha aur Surah nahi padh saka

✅ Kya Karo:

1️⃣ Takbeer kaho aur foran Imam ke saath ruku mein chale jao

2️⃣ Ruku mein Imam ke saath raho — yeh rakat MILI ✅

3️⃣ Agar sirf Sajde mein mila → woh rakat NAHI mili ❌ — baad mein woh khud padho

📌 Simple Rule:

Ruku mila = Rakat mili ✅
Sirf Sajda mila = Rakat nahi mili ❌ (woh baad mein khud padho)

💡 Masbuq Ka Golden Rule — Yaad Rakho

Rule 1: Imam Ke Saath Jo Padhi = Poori Mili ✅

Imam ke saath jo bhi rakat padhi, woh sahi maan loge — chahe kuch miss bhi hua ho

Rule 2: Imam Ke Salam Par Tum Mat Phero ✅

Imam salam pherta hai — tum uthte raho aur apni chhooti rakaat poori karo

Rule 3: Khud Padhi Rakaat Mein Surah Milao ✅

Jab khud apni chhooti rakat padho toh pehli 2 mein Fatiha + Surah dono padho

Rule 4: Baithna Wahan Karo Jahan Tumhari Original Rakat Thi ✅

Agar tumhari 2nd rakat poori ho rahi hai — toh 2nd ke baad Attahiyat ke liye baitho, Imam ne wahan baitha tha ya nahi.

🤔 Qasr Kya Hota Hai?

📖 "Qasr" = Namaz Ko Adha Karna (Qasra = Short Karna)

📌 Quick Rule:

🌆 Maghrib (3 Rakat) → Same rehti hai → 3 Rakat

📋 Qasr Ke Liye Conditions — Kab Karo?

✅ Condition 1: Safar Ki Doori

Apne sheher ya gaon se kam se kam 77-78 km (48 miles) door ja rahe ho — tabhi Qasr karo.

📱 Real Example:

Delhi se Agra jaana (200+ km) → Qasr karo ✅
Delhi mein hi doosri colony jaana (10 km) → Qasr mat karo ❌

✅ Condition 2: Niyat Ka Safar

Safar karne ki niyat honi chahiye. Bas ghoomne ke liye 10 minute bahar gaye toh Qasr nahi karte.

✅ Condition 3: 15 Din Se Kam Rukna

Agar us jagah 15 din ya zyada rukne ka irada hai — toh woh "Muqeem" (resident) ban jaoge — Qasr nahi karoge.

📱 Real Example:

Mumbai gaye 5 din ke liye → Qasr karo ✅
Mumbai gaye naukri par 1 mahine ke liye → Qasr mat karo ❌

✅ Qasr Namaz Kaise Padhe — Full Method

Jam' kya hai?

📱 Real Example — Road Trip:

🌙 Ramadan Kya Hai?

Ramadan Islamic calendar ka 9th mahina hai jisme Muslims poore din ka roza rakhte hain — subah Fajr se lekar shaam Maghrib tak kuch bhi khana-peena band. Ye mahina Quran ke nazil hone ka mahina hai aur sawab 70 guna zyada milta hai! Alhamdulillah! 🤲

🌅 Sehri — Roza Rakhne Ki Dua

Sehri Kya Hai?

Fajr se pehle jo khana khate hain use Sehri kehte hain. Ye Sunnat hai — Nabi ﷺ ne farmaya: "Sehri khao, kyunki sehri mein barkat hai." (Bukhari)

⏰ Sehri Ka Waqt

Subah Fajr ke azan se pehle sehri karni chahiye. Azan shuru hone se pehle khana-peena band kar lo. India mein approximate Fajr 4:30-5:30 AM hoti hai (season ke hisaab se).

🤲 Sehri Ke Waqt Niyat Ki Dua

🌇 Iftar — Roza Kholne Ki Duaein

Iftar Kya Hai?

Shaam ko Maghrib ki azan ke waqt roza kholna. Nabi ﷺ ne khajoor ya paani se iftar shuru karne ki Sunnat batai hai. Iftar ke waqt dua qubool hoti hai! 🤲

⭐ Main Iftar Dua (Mashoor)

Transliteration:

Allahumma inni laka sumtu wa bika aamantu wa alayka tawakkaltu wa ala rizq-ika-aftartu.

Matlab (Meaning):

"Aye Allah! Maine tere liye roza rakha, tujhpe iman laya, tujhpe bharosa kiya, aur teri diya hua rizq se roza khola."

📌 Kab Bolein?

Maghrib ki azan se pehle ya azan suntey hi — pehla ghunth paani ya khajoor lete waqt ye dua padho. 🌙

🤲 Iftar Ke Waqt Ki Dua (Abu Dawud)

Zahahaz-Zama'u Wabtallatil-'Urooqu Wa Thabatal-Ajru Insha-Allah.

"Pyaas bujh gayi, naadiyaan tar ho gayin aur Insha-Allah sawab pak ho gaya."

📌 Kab Bolein?

Roza kholne ke turant baad — paani peete waqt. Ye dua Nabi ﷺ ne khud padhi thi (Abu Dawud 2357).

🤲 Kisi Ko Iftar Karana Ke Baad Ki Dua

Aftara Indakumus-Saa'imoona, Wa Akala Ta'aamakumul-Abraaru, Wa Sallat 'Alaikumul-Malaa'ikah.

"Aapke paas rozadaaron ne roza khola, nekokaaron ne aapka khana khaya aur farishtey aap par darood bhejte hain."

📌 Kab Bolein?

Jab aap kisi ko iftar karaao (khana khilao) — toh ye dua padho. Mizbaan (host) ke liye ye dua hai.

🎉 Eid Ki Namaz — Complete Guide

Eid-ul-Fitr aur Eid-ul-Adha (Bakrid) — Saari Malumat

🌙 Eid-ul-Fitr Kyu Manate Hain?

🎯 Ramadan ke 30 rozon ke baad — Allah ka shukar ada karne ke liye Eid-ul-Fitr manate hain.

📅 Shawwal ka pehla din — Islamic calendar ke 10th mahine ki pehli tarikh ko Eid hoti hai.

📖 Quran ka hukm — Allah ne farmaya: "Roza poora karo aur Allah ki takbeer bolo taaki tum shukar guzar bano." (Baqarah: 185)

🕌 Nabi ﷺ ne bataya — Jab Nabi ﷺ Madina aye, wahan 2 festival the. Aapne farmaya: "Allah ne inke badle 2 behtar din diye — Eid-ul-Fitr aur Eid-ul-Adha." (Abu Dawud)

⏰ Eid Namaz Ka Waqt — Alag Alag Masjid Mein Alag Kyun?

📌 Official Timing:

Eid ki namaz ka waqt Ishraq ke baad shuru hota hai — yani jab suraj ek neza (taqreeban 15-20 minutes) upar aa jaye. Aur Zawal (dophar) se pehle khatam ho jaaye.

Waqt Approximate Time Status
Sunrise ke waqt ~6:00-6:30 AM ❌ Mana Hai
Ishraq ke baad ~6:45-7:00 AM ✅ Shuru
Afzal Waqt 7:00 - 9:00 AM ⭐ Best Time
Zawal Se Pehle ~11:30-12:00 PM ⚠️ Aakhri Waqt
Zawal ke baad 12:00+ PM ❌ Khatam

🤔 Alag Masjid Mein Alag Time Kyun?

🕌 Jagah ka masla: Eid ek saath poori city mein hoti hai — ek Eidgah mein sab fit nahi hote, is liye alag masjidein alag time rakhti hain.

👥 Crowd manage karna: Agar sab ek waqt ho, toh parking aur crowd ka masla hoga. Alag time se sab aasaan hota hai.

📢 Imam ki decision: Har Imam apni masjid ke hisaab se waqt decide karta hai — koi 7 AM, koi 8 AM, koi 9 AM rakhta hai.

🌍 Roza ki khatam: Kuch jagah Eid ka chand ek din pehle dikhta hai, kuch jagah baad — is wajah se bhi thoda fark hota hai.

🕌 Eid-ul-Fitr Namaz Ka Tareeqa

📋 Eid Ki Namaz Ka Khulasa:

2 Rakat — 6 Takbeeron ke saath (3 pehli rakat mein, 3 doosri mein). Imam Khutba deta hai Namaz ke baad.

1️⃣ Niyat Karo

"Main niyat karta/karti hoon 2 Rakat Eid-ul-Fitr ki Wajib Namaz ki, 6 Zaid Takbeeron ke saath, Imam ke peeche, wajah Allah ki, muh mera Qibla ki taraf."

2️⃣ Takbeer-e-Tahrima (Pehli Takbeer)

Haath kaano tak uthao, bolein "Allahu Akbar" — haath baandh lo. Sana padho (Subhanaka...).

3️⃣ Pehli Rakat — 3 Zaid Takbeerein

Sana ke baad Imam 3 baar "Allahu Akbar" kahega — har baar haath uthao, CHHODDO (haath mat baandho).

⚠️ 3rd takbeer ke baad haath baandh lo — phir Imam Surah Fatiha aur Surah padhega.

Takbeeron ke beech padhein (optional):

Subhanallahi Walhamdulillahi Wala Ilaha Illallahu Wallahu Akbar.

4️⃣ Normal Rakat — Ruku, Sajda etc.

Pehli rakat normal tareeqe se puri karo — Surah ke baad Ruku, 2 Sajde. Phir 2nd rakat ke liye khade ho jao.

5️⃣ Doosri Rakat — 3 Aur Takbeerein

2nd rakat mein Surah Fatiha + Surah ke baad — 3 Zaid Takbeerein bolein. Phir 4th takbeer ke saath Ruku mein jao.

Yaad rakhein 2nd Rakat Mein:

Surah padhne ke BAAD takbeerein → phir Ruku

6️⃣ Aakhri Baithak aur Salam

Normal tareeqe se — Attahiyat + Durood + Dua + Salam ✅

7️⃣ Imam Ki Khutba Suno

⚠️ Zaroor Yaad Rakhein:

Eid ki Khutba Namaz ke BAAD hoti hai (Jumah ki Namaz se alag!). Khutba sunna Wajib hai — beech mein mat uthna.

🕋 Hajj ki yaad mein: Eid-ul-Adha Hajj ke sath milti hai — Zul-Hijjah ke 10th din hoti hai.

🐏 Hazrat Ibrahim ؑ ki qurbani: Allah ne Hazrat Ibrahim ؑ ko apne bete Hazrat Ismail ؑ ki qurbani dene ka hukm diya. Unki wafadari dekh kar Allah ne bête ki jagah bakra bheja! Isi yaad mein qurbani karte hain.
